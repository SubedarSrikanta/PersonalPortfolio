Food Review Website

A Food Review Website is an online platform where users can share their dining experiences by reviewing and rating restaurants. It helps others make informed decisions by offering insights on food quality, service, ambiance, and pricing. Users can search for restaurants based on location, cuisine, or price, and view detailed reviews and ratings. Each restaurant has a profile with key information, including menus and photos. The platform fosters a community-driven approach to dining recommendations, allowing users to connect, share, and discover new dining experiences while helping restaurants improve their offerings.

Simple Calculator

The Simple Calculator I created is a user-friendly web-based tool designed to perform essential arithmetic operations such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it features an intuitive interface with clearly labeled buttons for digits and operators, ensuring ease of use. Users can input numbers, perform calculations, and receive results instantly. The calculator also includes functions for clearing inputs and resetting the screen. Its minimalistic design ensures functionality without distractions, making it ideal for quick calculations in everyday tasks. The responsive layout ensures accessibility across different devices, from desktops to mobile screens.
